Add a Recipe

Add Recipe Information

To add a new recipe, you must first specify the information for the recipe:

  • Your recipe needs a catchy name where you and other users can find it easily.
  • Your recipe needs a good description so people following it know what they are in for.
  • Your recipe should specify an accurate prep and cook time for the recipe; time management is important!

Add Ingredients

Now, we must include the ingredients to make your masterpiece!

  • Enter ingredients on a separate line in the field below.
  • To ensure users can change serving size when viewing the recipe, enter ingredients in the following format: ex. 1 cup flour

Add Steps

Let's show everyone interested in your recipe how to make it!

  • Enter steps on a separate line in the field below, just like you did for ingredients.

Make sure you double-check your creation!

When you are ready, you can submit.
